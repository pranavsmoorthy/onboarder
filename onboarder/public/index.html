Welcome to Onboarder

Onboarder.com takes the headache out of employee onboarding, offering a comprehensive platform that streamlines the process for both new hires and HR teams. Its intuitive interface makes it easy for companies to design customized onboarding journeys that perfectly suit their needs. This means new hires can experience a tailored program that gets them up to speed quickly and feeling welcome.

Who are We?

Onboarder.com goes beyond just the mechanics. It fosters a positive and engaging experience for new hires. Imagine receiving personalized welcome messages, completing interactive training modules, and having access to clear communication tools throughout the process. This sets the stage for higher engagement and a smoother transition into their new role.


Here's how Onboarder.com simplifies onboarding:


  • Build personalized onboarding journeys that cater to different departments, roles, or even individual needs. No more one-size-fits-all approaches!
  • Free up HR teams by automating repetitive tasks like sending welcome emails, or assigning training modules.
  • Gain real-time insights into how new hires are progressing through their onboarding journey. Identify any roadblocks and ensure everyone stays on track.
